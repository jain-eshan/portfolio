About

Product Manager, designer, and builder. I think in systems, build with taste, and ship with urgency.

I've spent the last 4+ years learning how to turn fuzzy problems into products people actually use. That journey started at University of Delhi, where I studied commerce but spent most of my time building — founding a startup incubator, running cohorts, and figuring out what makes products stick.

Today I'm a Product Manager at Visit Health, leading pods across cashless physiotherapy, pre-policy medical checkups, and medical underwriting. Before that, I was a Product Associate at Favcy Venture Builders, working across early-stage portfolio companies.

On the side, I co-founded Fitin Club — a lifestyle brand that makes fitness and nutrition approachable. We've run paid cohorts, built recommendation engines, and been featured in Nikhil Kamath's WTF Fund.

I'm incoming at ISB's Young Leaders Program (Class of 2027) and deeply curious about the intersection of product thinking and AI-assisted building. This entire portfolio was built without writing a single line of code manually — it's all vibe coded with Claude.
