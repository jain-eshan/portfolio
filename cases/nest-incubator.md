## The Problem

Students at SGGSCC had ideas but no structured support to go from concept to prototype. No incubator existed at the college level to provide mentorship, resources, and accountability.

## What I Did

- Founded and led the incubator from scratch during my B.Com years
- Built a mentorship pipeline connecting students with industry professionals
- Designed the selection, cohort, and demo-day processes
- Incubated 10+ student ventures over 2.5 years

## Results

10+ ventures incubated, sustainable operations model that continues after graduation, multiple ventures that went on to external funding.
