### ***Context***
If I were to ask any health professional about the most trending and profitable avenues or specialities right now, their answer would probably be one among Physiotherapy or Dermatology and I was charged with building out both of these verticals during my stint at Visit Health as a product manager.

With the rise in OPD cover in health/life insurance and increasing elective procedures, doctors and surgeons are prescribing physiotherapy as a wellness/recovery/healing process to most of their patients and this made it imperative for Visit health to have Cashless Physiotherapy as an offering to its corporate clients and retail insurers who are dying to offer this as VAS (Value added services) to their stakeholders/customers.
