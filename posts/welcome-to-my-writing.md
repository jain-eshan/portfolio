I've been meaning to write publicly for years. Not the LinkedIn kind — the slow, honest kind. The kind you come back to a year later and realise how much you've changed.

This is that corner.

## What you'll find here

Notes on building products at **Visit Health**, lessons from shipping side projects like [Fitin Club](https://fitin.club) and [Reccos](https://reccos.fitin.club), the occasional rant about design systems, and a lot of thinking in public about what it means to be a non-technical founder in 2026.

## How this is built

Every post you read here started as a markdown file in my Obsidian vault. I write. I hit save. A tiny script notices the new file and my website rebuilds itself overnight.

No CMS. No editor UI. Just text → git → live.

That's the kind of plumbing that makes writing feel lightweight again.
