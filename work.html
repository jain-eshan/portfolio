Fitin Club

A lifestyle brand making fitness & nutrition stick. Flexible, guided, judgment-free.

Co-founder Product Design Growth
The Problem
Most fitness products intimidate beginners. They assume motivation, discipline, and gym access. We wanted to build something that meets people where they are — at home, in 15 minutes, without judgment.
What I Did
  • Designed and shipped the end-to-end product — from onboarding to workout delivery
  • Built cohort-based programs (Habit Cohort) that drove 50+ paid members at ₹1,000 each
  • Ran growth experiments across Instagram, WhatsApp, and referral loops
  • Featured in Nikhil Kamath's WTF Fund for habit-building innovation
Results
50+ paying members, WTF Fund recognition, community-first growth model that sustains without paid ads.

Reccos by Fitin

Discover clean, high-quality food products. Built on Lovable in a weekend.

Solo Builder Product Design
The Problem
Our Fitin community kept asking "which protein powder should I buy?" and "is this brand actually clean?" — there was no curated, trusted source for healthy food recommendations tailored to Indian consumers.
What I Did
  • Built the entire product solo using Lovable (React + Vite + Supabase + shadcn/ui)
  • Designed the curation flow, product cards, and filtering system
  • Sourced and verified initial product catalog from the community
Results
Shipped in a weekend. Became the go-to recommendation engine for the Fitin community.

This Portfolio

An interactive canvas-style portfolio built entirely with AI tools. No manual coding.

Product Design Vibe Coded
The Problem
Most PM portfolios are static and forgettable. I wanted something that demonstrates how I think — interactive, opinionated, and built with the same tools I advocate for.
What I Did
  • Designed the entire UX: collaboration mode, draggable elements, cursor tour, canvas-style layout

  • Dark mode, responsive, accessible — all shipped in days, not weeks
Results
Shipped v1 to v3 in under a week. Living proof that non-technical founders can build production-quality products with AI.
